# Explainable AI (XAI) Framework – IntelliStore AI

Every recommendation generated by any AI Agent in **IntelliStore AI** must be transparent, measurable, explainable, and actionable. The platform must **NEVER** produce unexplained recommendations or function as a black box.

---

## 1. Business Justification Engine (10 Mandatory Questions)

Before generating any recommendation, every AI Agent must evaluate and answer the following 10 questions. If a recommendation cannot answer all 10 questions with measurable data, it is rejected and will not be presented to users.

1. **What problem currently exists?**  
   *(Example: 2,450 project files from 2024 are residing on high-cost HOT tier NVMe storage.)*
2. **Why is this a problem?**  
   *(Example: Inactive data on HOT tiers consumes expensive IOPS and capacity, inflating monthly billing by $180.50.)*
3. **How was this problem detected?**  
   *(Example: Nightly access timestamp scanning revealed zero read/write operations over the past 18 months.)*
4. **What evidence supports the finding?**  
   *(Example: Access logs show 0 read events; file metadata confirms average file age > 540 days.)*
5. **What recommendation is being proposed?**  
   *(Example: Migrate the 2,450 inactive files (6.2 TB total) from HOT tier to COLD archive storage.)*
6. **Why is this recommendation appropriate?**  
   *(Example: COLD storage costs $0.004/GB/mo compared to HOT storage at $0.023/GB/mo, perfectly aligning with infrequent access patterns.)*
7. **What measurable benefits will be achieved?**  
   *(Example: Immediate monthly savings of $180.50 ($2,166/year) and 6.2 TB of reclaimed high-speed NVMe storage.)*
8. **What are the possible risks?**  
   *(Example: If an archived file is needed unexpectedly, retrieval latency increases from <50ms to ~3–5 seconds.)*
9. **Can this recommendation be safely rolled back?**  
   *(Example: Yes. One-click rollback restores files back to the HOT tier within 60 seconds.)*
10. **How confident is the AI?**  
   *(Example: 96% confidence based on statistical access probability and historical tiering patterns.)*

## 2. Recommendation Schema & UI Panel ("Why This Recommendation?")

Every recommendation card displayed in the Executive Dashboard or Storage Copilot must provide a complete **"Why This Recommendation?"** breakdown:

```yaml
Recommendation Title: Move 120 GB to Cold Storage
Reason: These files have not been accessed for 18 months and are consuming premium storage.
Business Value: Estimated annual savings: ₹14,200 ($170.00 USD)
Storage Saved: 120 GB
Confidence: 96%
Priority: High
Responsible Agent: Cost Optimization Agent
Estimated Completion Time: 4 Minutes
Rollback: Available (1-Click Restore)

Supporting Evidence:
  - Last Access Date: 2024-11-14
  - Average Access Frequency: 0.00 read/month
  - Storage Tier: HOT (NVMe)
  - Current Storage Cost: $2.76 / month
  - Predicted Future Access Probability: < 1.2%
  - Expected Storage Savings: 100% of HOT tier allocation
```

## 3. Enterprise Decision Support & 360° Reasoning

To assist enterprise decision-makers (CFOs, Storage Admins, CISOs), every AI recommendation synthesizes five distinct dimensions of reasoning:

* **Business Reasoning**: Quantifies ROI, budget impact, and resource efficiency.
* **Technical Reasoning**: Details IOPS reduction, storage tier saturation, and deduplication math.
* **Financial Reasoning**: Outlines exact monthly and annual cost deltas across storage pricing tiers.
* **Operational Reasoning**: Estimates administrator time saved and automated workflow execution speed.
* **Security & Compliance Reasoning**: Identifies PII exposure reduction, heuristic malware containment, and retention policy alignment.
